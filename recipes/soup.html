Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup is a comforting, hearty dish made with simmered chicken, vegetables, and aromatic herbs. The broth is rich and flavorful, often enhanced with ingredients like carrots, celery, and onions. Perfect for chilly days or when you're feeling under the weather, chicken soup is known for its soothing qualities and nourishing properties.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole or parts)
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
