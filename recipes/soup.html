Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup is a comforting, hearty dish made with simmered chicken, vegetables, and aromatic herbs. The broth is rich and flavorful, often enhanced with ingredients like carrots, celery, and onions. Perfect for chilly days or when you're feeling under the weather, chicken soup is known for its soothing qualities and nourishing properties.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole or parts)
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken: In a large pot, add the chicken, broth (or water), and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes until cooked through.
  2. Remove Chicken: Take the chicken out and let it cool slightly. Shred the meat, discarding the skin and bones.
  3. Saut√© Vegetables: In the same pot, add carrots, celery, onion, and garlic. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until softened.
  4. Add Chicken and Season: Return the shredded chicken to the pot. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme or parsley. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes.
