Anpan Bread Recipe

Source: justonecookbook.com

Its time to use the Yudane bread dough to something more varied. Anpan is a hamburger-like bun bread with a red bean filling which is a favorite in East Asia.

Ingredients:

  • Yudane : 28gr
  • Flour : 172gr
  • Water : 120gr
  • Yeast : 3gr
  • Salt : 4gr
  • Sugar : 12gr
  • Butter : 9gr
  • Margarine : 9gr
  • Egg : 1 Yolk
  • Red Bean Filling: 100gr

Steps needed

  1. Mix in water, yeast, salt, sugar, & yudane. Stir to autolyse.
  2. Insert the flour and yolk, mix it all together until no more dry flour left.
  3. incorporate the butter into the dough by folding it within the bread.
  4. Knead the bread until it doesn't stick anymore using the slap & fold method.
  5. Let thee dough rest for about an hour, fold in the first 30 minutes.
  6. Divide the dough into equal pieces and shape it into a ball. Let it rest for an hour
  7. Flatten the doughs, and insert the red bean filling in the middle. Close it by folding it like a dumpling. Let it then rest for another hour.
  8. coat the top part of the bread with an egg yolk milk mix
  9. Preheat your oven and bake the bread for 30-50 minutes.
