Chicken Teriyaki

Credit: onceuponachef.com

Description

Chicken teriyaki is a popular Japanese dish featuring tender, marinated chicken cooked in a savory-sweet sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. The chicken is often grilled or stir-fried until caramelized and served over rice, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions. Its delightful balance of flavors makes it a favorite in many households.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin (or rice vinegar)
  • Sugar (or honey)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Marinade: In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, mirin, sugar, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Marinate Chicken: Place the chicken in a resealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours in the fridge).
  3. Cook Chicken: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from the marinade (reserve the marinade) and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Make Sauce: In the same skillet, add the reserved marinade and bring it to a boil. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Serve: Slice the chicken and drizzle with the sauce. Serve over rice and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
